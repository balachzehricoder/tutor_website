About us

The Origin of Perth Tutors

Perth Tutors WA emerged as a response to a modern challenge: busy parents struggling to find the right tutor for their child in a saturated tutoring market.

Our inception was driven by a clear vision – to simplify the tutor selection process while ensuring that each student receives personalized attention suited to their unique learning needs.

Our Unique Approach

Our unique approach sets us apart in the educational landscape. We offer a complimentary 15-minute online assessment or discussion, allowing us to understand each student's academic level and specific requirements.

This initial step is crucial in our mission to provide tailored tutoring services that are not just effective but also enjoyable and impactful.

Tutor Matching Process

Our role as an experienced tutor finder is pivotal. With the insights gained from the initial assessment, we delve into our extensive network of accredited tutors to find the perfect match for each student.
This ensures a harmonious learning experience, where the tutor's teaching style aligns seamlessly with the student's learning preferences.

How We Select The Tutors

1. Rigorous Screening

The first step in our tutor selection process is rigorous screening. We review each tutor's educational background, teaching credentials, and professional experience. This thorough evaluation ensures that only highly qualified and competent tutors are considered for our network.

2. Skill Assessment

After passing the initial screening, potential tutors undergo a comprehensive skill assessment. This includes evaluating their teaching methodologies, subject matter expertise, and ability to adapt to different learning styles. This step is crucial to ascertain their capability to deliver personalized and effective tutoring.

3. Personal Interview

The third step involves a personal interview. Here, we assess the tutor's communication skills, passion for teaching, and commitment to student success. We also evaluate their ability to build rapport with students, which is vital for creating a positive and conducive learning environment.

4. Continuous Evaluation

Lastly, we have a system of continuous evaluation for our tutors. Regular feedback from students and parents plays a significant role in this process. It allows us to monitor the tutor's performance, ensuring they consistently meet our high standards and positively impact student learning.

Commitment to Excellence

Perth Tutors’ vision empowers parents to feel confident about their child's academic journey, knowing they are in expert hands. We take pride in offering both online and in-person tutoring, ensuring flexibility and convenience.

Our goal is to make education a fulfilling and enriching experience for every student.

Our Promise

We promise personalized, fun, and impactful tutoring sessions. Perth Tutors WA is more than a tutoring service; it's a partner in your child's educational journey, ensuring their academic needs are met with the highest standards of excellence.
